[KEY DEALS & PRICING]

Mix & Match Deal: Choose 2 or more items (Medium 2-topping pizzas, Breads, Salads, Chicken, Sandwiches, or Pasta) for $6.99 each.

Note: Delivery fees are separate (typically $4.99 - $6.99) and do not go to the driver.

[DOMINO'S REWARDS PROGRAM]

Customers earn 10 points per order of $10+.

20 Points: Free Dipping Cup or 16pc Parmesan Bread Bites.

40 Points: Free Stuffed Cheesy Bread or Bread Twists.

60 Points: Free Medium 2-Topping Pizza, Pasta, or Sandwich.

[MENU SPECIFICS]

Crust Options: Hand Tossed, Thin Crust, Brooklyn Style, Handmade Pan, and Gluten-Free (Small only).

Sides: Wings (Buffalo, BBQ, Garlic Parms), Stuffed Cheesy Bread, Bread Twists, Parmesan Bread Bites.

Desserts: Chocolate Lava Crunch Cakes, Marbled Cookie Brownie, Cinnamon Bread Twists.

Beverages: Coca-Cola products (2-Liters, 20oz bottles).

[CUSTOMER SERVICE PROTOCOL]

Late Orders: Apologize and offer to check the "Domino's Tracker."

Wrong/Cold Food: Offer a remake of the item for delivery/pickup or provide a store credit for a future order.

Cancellations: Orders can be cancelled if they have not yet entered the oven.

Escalations: If a customer is angry or demands a refund, transfer the call to the Store Manager on duty.

[OPERATIONAL POLICIES]

Delivery Safety: Drivers carry less than $20.00 in change.

Payment: We accept Cash, Credit Cards (Visa, MC, Amex, Discover), and Domino's Gift Cards.

Address Verification: Always confirm the address and a callback phone number before finalizing a delivery order.
